### Customer (Non-Technical Founder)

- **Readability:** A founder *could* walk the artifacts in order and understand "what got built and why." Brainstorm explains decisions; spec defines requirements; threat model is short enough; release notes are clear.
- **Scope honesty:** The post-fix spec title "Verified-Code-to-Shipped Pipeline" + scope note explicitly says "non-technical founders are out of scope." A founder reading this knows immediately whether to walk away or invest. That honesty is welcome.
- **Where a founder would still hit a wall:** Dafny is still required for `[verifiable-model]` REQs (the founder cannot read or write `.dfy` files). - **Verdict:** The demo correctly demonstrates a *technical* product, not a *founder* product. The factory-lite gap is acknowledged with a concrete commitment. Sign off.
